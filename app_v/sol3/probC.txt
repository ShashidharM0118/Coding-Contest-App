<b>Problem:</b> <span foreground='blue'>Find the Greatest Divisor</span>

<b>Description:</b>
Given a positive integer <b>n</b> (2 ≤ n ≤ 10^9), find the greatest divisor(x) of the number n, where x not equal to n .

<b>Input:</b>
The input consists of a single line containing a positive integer <b>n</b>.

<b>Output:</b>
Output a single integer, the greatest divisor <b>x</b> of <b>n</b>.

<b>Sample Input:</b>
36
<b>Sample Output:</b>
18
