<b>Problem:</b> <span foreground='blue'>Carl's Addition Adventure</span>

<b>Description:</b>
Given two positive integers <b>x</b> and <b>y</b> (10^-9 ≤ x, y ≤ 10^9), Carl wants to find the sum of these two numbers.

<b>Input:</b>
The input consists of two lines, each containing a single integer, <b>x</b> and <b>y</b>.

<b>Output:</b>
Output a single integer, the sum of <b>x</b> and <b>y</b>.

<b>Sample Input:</b>
12345
67890

<b>Sample Output:</b>
80235
